## Notes

- **Nreal** is the former name of **XREAL** — the Nreal Light photo represents the
  AR-glasses category, not the XREAL 1S / Viture Beast / Luma Ultra specifically.
- The Pixel render is the **9 Pro**; the 10 Pro XL and 11 Pro XL share the family design.
- The Microsoft keyboard is a **bi-fold** example; the ProtoArc XK01 TP is a **tri-fold**
  with an integrated trackpad.

Per **CC BY-SA**, these images remain under their original licenses. If you reuse them,
keep the attribution and share-alike terms. To replace any with the exact models, drop
your **own photos** of owned gear into `static/images/products/` and update the figure
captions.
